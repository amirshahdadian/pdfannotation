# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be professional and considerate.
- Focus on technical issues, not people.
- Give actionable feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Deliberate disruption or bad-faith behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit/remove comments or contributions that violate this policy and may restrict participation for repeated violations.
